## Day 10 – Team Leadership & Communication

Today I tried to lead my mini-project team as a team lead.

What I did:
- Took initiative to start the discussion
- Assigned tasks based on strengths
- Listened to team members’ opinions
- Tried to keep everyone involved

What I learned:
- Leadership is about coordination, not control
- Clear communication reduces confusion
- Listening builds trust in a team

Challenges:
- Managing different opinions
- Keeping the discussion focused

Next improvement:
- Prepare a clear plan before meetings
- Communicate decisions confidently
